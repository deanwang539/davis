Background

In the titration lab, you will learn the importance of titrations by titrating an acid with a base. Titrations have many practical uses. In medicine, titrations are used to create exact mixtures of drugs for intravenous drips. Additionally, they are used in pregnancy testing and blood glucose meters. Titrations often have uses in waste removal and biodiesel production by determining the acidity of a variety of solutions. -Some famous Source
